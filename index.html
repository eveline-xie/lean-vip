What is lean six sigma?

It is the implementation of various methods, and tools to control organizational processes.

What we do

The objective is to eliminate waste, improve efficiency and minimize process variations throughout the organization. We believe that by having a student team focusing on issues encountered within your department using Lean Six Sigma tools and processes in order to solve (and even improve) processes within your department, we will help your bottom line while both parties have a learning experience as to how Lean Six Sigma can be implemented within your department.
